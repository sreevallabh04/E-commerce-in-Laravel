Our Story

Founded in 2000, LuxFurn has been at the forefront of luxury furniture design and manufacturing. We believe that furniture is not just functional - it's an expression of your personality and lifestyle.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create furniture that combines timeless elegance with modern functionality. Today, we're proud to be one of India's leading luxury furniture brands, serving discerning customers across the globe.
